Manual

  • Personal Details: Click the Person Up Float button to open a modal, fill in your details, and click "Add." Unfilled fields won't appear in the invoice.
  • Client Details: Click the Person Down Float button to open a modal, fill in your client's details, and click "Add." Unfilled fields won't appear in the invoice.
  • Add Invoice Items: Click the File Add button to open a modal and add your invoice items.
  • Edit/Delete Items: After adding items, click the respective cells in the invoice table to edit or delete them.
  • Add Terms: Click "Terms" to open an alert box, add your terms, and click the created terms to delete them.
  • Add Notes: Click "Note" to open an alert box, add your notes, and click the created notes to delete them.
